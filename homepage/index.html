How it works

Learn why

This program was born with a goal: to connect you to live information about past and future disasters, so you can keep what matters most to you safe and sound.

We gather data on seismic activites that is made public by NASA, correctly identify and remove the noise, keeping only the useful data, and use this to feed a neural network specialized in predicting future large-scale seismic events.

With this, we can create the prediction models you saw above, and successfully prevent the massive loss of life and resources that generally comes with such tragedies, even on other planets, when space travel comes to be.

As an added bonus, the noise suppresion, which is implemented in the seismic sensors themselves, stops the equipment to transmit useless data back to Earth, saving on power and extending their mission duration.
